MORE ABOUT TREASURE

Treasure is a boygroup from YG Entertainment. Debut on August 7th, 2020 with single Boy. This group formed through survival show YG Treasure Box. Treasure has debut with 13 members.

On November 2022 Bang Yedam and Mashiho left the group. And now Treasure continue with 10 members ; Choi Hyunsuk, Park Jihoon, Yoshi, Kim Junkyu, Yoon jaehyuk, Asahi, Kim Doyoung, Haruto, Park Jeongwoo and So Junghwan.
